Symmetry

Symmetry Formulas & Definitions

Group Order

Formula: $$ h = \sum_{i=1}^{n} n_i $$

Definitions:

  • h: Order of the group (total number of symmetry operations)
  • ni: Number of operations in the i-th class

Character Orthogonality

Formula: $$ \sum_{R} \chi^{(i)}(R) \chi^{(j)}(R)^* = h \delta_{ij} $$

  • χ(i)(R): Character of operation R in representation i
  • χ(j)(R): Character in representation j
  • R: Symmetry operation
  • h: Group order
  • δij: Kronecker delta (1 if i = j, 0 otherwise)

Reduction of Representations

Formula: $$ a_i = \frac{1}{h} \sum_{R} n_R \chi^{\text{red}}(R) \chi^{(i)}(R) $$

  • ai: Number of times irreducible representation i appears
  • χred(R): Character of reducible representation
  • χ(i)(R): Character of irreducible representation i
  • nR: Number of operations in the class of R
  • h: Group order

Great Orthogonality Theorem

Formula: $$ \sum_{R} D^{(i)}_{mn}(R) D^{(j)}_{pq}(R)^* = \frac{h}{l_i} \delta_{ij} \delta_{mp} \delta_{nq} $$

  • D(i)mn(R): Matrix element m,n of representation i under R
  • h: Group order
  • li: Dimension of representation i
  • δ: Kronecker delta

Normal Mode Count

Formula: $$ 3N = T + R + V $$

  • N: Number of atoms
  • T: Translational degrees (3 total)
  • R: Rotational degrees (2 or 3)
  • V: Vibrational degrees of freedom

Symmetry Species of Motions

Formula: $$ \Gamma = \sum_{R} \chi(R) R $$

  • Γ: Total representation of motion
  • χ(R): Character under operation R
  • R: Symmetry operation

Symmetry Elements

  • E: Identity
  • Cn: Proper rotation
  • σ: Mirror plane (v = vertical, h = horizontal, d = diagonal)
  • i: Inversion center
  • Sn: Improper rotation (rotation + reflection)
